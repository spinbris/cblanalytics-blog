Andrej Karpathy gave us "vibe coding." I'm calling what I do "vibe analytics."

Talking to AI to research analytical project ideas:
→ Rationality Check - am I off my tree?
→ What has been done to date?
→ Is there enough data?
→ What approaches might work?
→ Only then - what toolsets? Could be an app... might just be a spreadsheet
→ Let's build it!

For finance professionals and analysts - this is our moment. AI can work very quickly, not just to do the analysis but to build in all the checks and balances required (thinking especially of those 10-15 year old spreadsheets in every finance process 😱).

The person asking the smartest questions can now build the answers.
